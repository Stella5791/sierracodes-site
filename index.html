About Sierra Codes

Sierra Codes is the digital studio of Stella Bullo — a linguist-turned-developer crafting tools at the intersection of language, code, and lived experience. Whether building metaphor-powered health apps or exploring multilingual NLP, Sierra Codes blends insight, empathy, and logic to make complex experiences more visible.

About Stella

I’m Stella Bullo — a linguist, developer, and writer based between Argentina and the UK. After two decades of research and teaching in UK higher education, I’ve shifted focus to building digital tools that give language power, clarity, and emotional resonance.

I founded Sierra Codes to bring together my deep expertise in discourse, metaphor, and health communication with a growing skill set in Python, NLP, and frontend development. My projects — from pain communication apps to bilingual content tools — are driven by a simple goal: to make the invisible visible.

I work in both English and Spanish. My work has been featured in The New York Times, BBC Radio, and peer-reviewed journals. I'm especially passionate about developing tools for underrepresented voices in health and research spaces.

Sierra Codes is my studio, my lighthouse, and my next chapter. Thanks for stopping by.
